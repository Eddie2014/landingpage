EVENT SCHEDULE

FRIDAY, MAY 5, 2017

AT CONCORD PACIFIC

FROM 5PM TO 8PM


2017 HUBLOT DIAMOND RALLY AWARDS CATEGORIES

Private reception for all registered drivers, navigators, accredited media and supercar buyers.
ADESA Richmond supercar auction with 20 vehicles (including 10 with no reserve) for sale.
Concord Pacific Presentation Centre located at 88 Pacific Blvd.
Pick up your decals and meet with your fellow Hublot Diamond Rally 2017 drivers.
After 8pm, dinner at La Pentola at your own leisure.
Late night imbibing at Bar None

SATURDAY, MAY 6, 2017

VANCOUVER TO WHISTLER AND BEYOND


8:00AM TO 9:30AM OFFICIAL START LOCATION BRIAN JESSEL BMW (2311 BOUNDARY ROAD) *OUTSIDE PUBLIC ACCESS*

Mandatory driver and navigator check-in: Submit waiver form Receive your decals Pick up lunch boxes Pick up wristbands and hats Continental breakfast for all registered drivers and navigators provided by Brian Jessel BMW.

9:30AM TO 9:45AM

Welcome Ceremony Sponsor welcomes Safety driver meeting

10:00AM - SHARP - DEAPRTURE!

Official rally start and departure via Second Narrows bridge onto Sea to Sky highway 99.

11:00AM - 1ST PIT STOP CHANCES CASINO (9000 VALLEY DRIVE) SQUAMISH *PUBLIC ACCESS

1-hour stop Gaming floor is open for you during this pit stop. Photo and Video opportunity, BBQ will be available for purchase. Depart at Noon - Sharp!



1:00PM - 2ND PIT STOP PEMBERTON AIRPORT (1850 AIRPORT ROAD) *NO PUBLIC ACCESS*

3-hour stop Supercar runway drives for all drivers who would like to participate. Photo and Video opportunity.

4:00PM DEPART TO FOUR SEASONS RESORT WHISTLER HOTEL

Depart from Pemberton Airport

4:30PM TO 6:00PM 3RD PIT STOP AWARDS PRESENTATION FOUR SEASONS RESORT HOTEL

Hublot Diamond Rally awards and cocktail reception in the private courtyard at the Four Seasons Resort Hotel for all drivers and navigators. 25 "Diamond Parking" passes will be allocated based on the automotive pedigree of your car. Allocated supercars will be parked outside the Four Seasons Resort Hotel.

11:00PM

Gather after dinner at Buffalo Bills for nightcaps and dancing with DJ music. Your Hublot Diamond Rally wristband will allow access into the club before 11pm for free. Enter through the VIP entrance.
